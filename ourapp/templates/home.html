The Value of Nature 


        

            The value of nature is that it provides us with a sense of peace, serenity, and comfort. Nature has helped us to survive as a species for millions of years. It has provided us with food, shelter, and many other things that we need to live. We cannot live without nature because it provides us with everything we need. We should understand that without nature there will be no space for us to live.So think about it this way, if you could choose one thing from nature that would help you be happier and healthier than ever before… what would it be?
